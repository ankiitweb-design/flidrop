What is Flidrop?

Flidrop is a free web app that transfers files directly between any two devices using your browser — no app to install, no account to create, and nothing ever uploaded to a server. Open Flidrop on both devices, share a 5-letter code, and your photos, videos, or any file travels straight across.

Works between any devices

Send files from iPhone to Android, Android to Windows PC, Mac to iPhone, phone to laptop — any combination works as long as both devices have a browser and are connected to the internet or the same Wi-Fi.

Why is Flidrop fast and private?

Flidrop uses WebRTC, the same peer-to-peer technology behind video calls. Files travel directly from one device to the other without passing through any server. The Flidrop server only helps the two devices find each other — it never sees, stores, or logs your files.

Free, no account needed

Flidrop is completely free. There is no sign-up, no login, and no storage limit. It works on any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — on any device.
